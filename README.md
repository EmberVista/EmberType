EmberType is a native macOS application that transforms your voice into text instantly. 100% offline, 100% private.

![EmberType Screenshot](https://embertype.com/embertype-screenshot.png)

## Features

- **100% Offline** — Your voice never leaves your Mac. All processing happens locally.
- **Whisper AI** — Powered by OpenAI's state-of-the-art speech recognition, optimized for Apple Silicon.
- **100+ Languages** — Transcribe and translate speech in over 100 languages.
- **Global Hotkeys** — Configurable keyboard shortcuts for instant dictation from anywhere.
- **Power Mode** — Intelligent app detection applies your preferred settings automatically.
- **Context Aware** — Smart AI understands your screen content and adapts.
- **Personal Dictionary** — Train the AI with your unique terminology and custom replacements.
- **AI Enhancement** — Built-in modes for different writing styles and contexts.
